telepathy, what is it?

telepathy is the ability to tune into your consciousness and any other background information coming to you in the form of thought. Telepathy is a skill just like carpentry or playing instruments. The more you practice, the better you'll be at hearing the "unknown". To actually use telepathy, you need to exit the beta state of your mind, and enter the alpha state. the beta state is your normal awake state, you can enter the alpha state when day dreaming, or doing repititive tasks. You can also enter the alpha state when making art, or coloring something like a mandala. I enter the alpha state by meditating. Once you are in this state, you may hallucinate, or hear things that makes you look around you, or ask "did you hear that?" when doing this try to distance yourself from electronics, even the electricity running through your home can effect your mind. all in all, just pay attention to the things in the back of your mind, observe your thoughts.
