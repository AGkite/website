Faster Than Light Travel

Introduction

Faster than light travel may seem impossible now thanks to eintein and his equations such as E=MC^2, but contrary to popular belief it is indeed very possible. There are a few methods of traveling faster than the speed of light actually. In this post I hope to make those possibilities and theorys seem more like a reality to you.

alcubierre drive

This device was theorized by a man named miguel alcubierre. the craft would be possible by creating an energy density field lower than that of a vacuum. so basically you need negative mass. Rather than exceeding the speed of light within a relative frame of reference, it would work by contracting space in front of it, and expanding the space behind it. This warp drive is also consistent with Einsteins field equations. It might be meaningless though. It requires an exotic matter with negative energy density. It is argued by alcubierre that the casimir vacuum could fullfill the negative energy requirement.
