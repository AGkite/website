Guliana city location

Guliana city, not to be confused with Guiliana CIty, is a hidden city located somewhere between malaysia and the indian ocean. The small city was built on an island back in the early 1900s during World War 1. Used as a safe haven of sorts. the only people left in the city are direct decedents of those that took refuge back in 1914-1918.

Despite the small amount of people rumored to still reside in the hidden city. what onc was an extravagant island of sorts is now nothing but ruins. The local residents manage to live off the economy provided by the island. Some believe Guliana is nothing but a destroyed and dangerous city better left alone.
